General Questions

Medisync is a comprehensive digital healthcare platform that connects patients with qualified doctors, enables online consultations, manages health records, and provides emergency services. We offer a seamless healthcare experience through our integrated system.

Click on "Patient Login" or "Doctor Login" in the navigation menu, then select "Sign Up" or "Create Account". Fill in your details including name, email, phone number, and create a secure password. You'll receive a verification email to activate your account.

Yes! Our emergency services and support hotline are available 24/7. Regular appointments can be scheduled during working hours (Mon-Fri: 8 AM - 8 PM, Sat: 9 AM - 5 PM, Sun: 9 AM - 2 PM), but our online platform is accessible anytime.

Appointments & Booking

Navigate to the "Book Appointment" page, select your preferred specialty and doctor, choose an available date and time slot, fill in your details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive instant confirmation via email and SMS.

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time through your patient dashboard. For cancellations within 24 hours, please call our support line at (555) 123-4567.

Please notify us immediately if you're running late. We'll do our best to accommodate you, but appointments may need to be rescheduled if you're more than 15 minutes late to ensure fairness to other patients.

Yes, you can add family members to your account and book appointments on their behalf. Each family member will have their own medical records and appointment history.

Telemedicine & Online Consultations

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all video consultations and comply with HIPAA regulations. Your medical data is stored securely and is only accessible to you and your authorized healthcare providers.

You need a device (smartphone, tablet, or computer) with a camera, microphone, and stable internet connection. We recommend using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari browsers for the best experience.

Yes, doctors can issue e-prescriptions during online consultations. These are sent directly to your preferred pharmacy or available for download in your patient portal.

Billing & Insurance

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, digital wallets (PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay), bank transfers, and most insurance plans. Payment plans are also available for certain services.

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Please check with our billing department or your insurance company to verify coverage. You can also submit insurance information during appointment booking.

All receipts and invoices are automatically generated and available in your patient dashboard under "Billing History". You can download or email them directly from there.

Medical Records & Privacy

Yes! Once you log into your patient portal, you have 24/7 access to all your medical records, prescriptions, lab results, imaging reports, and consultation history. You can download or share them securely with other healthcare providers.

All medical records are stored in secure, HIPAA-compliant cloud servers with enterprise-grade encryption. We use role-based access control, ensuring only authorized healthcare providers can view your information.

Yes, you can securely share your medical records with any healthcare provider through our platform. You control who has access and can revoke permissions at any time.

Emergency Services

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency coordination services. Call our emergency hotline at 911 or (555) 123-4567 for immediate assistance. Our team will coordinate with the nearest medical facility for rapid response.

For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 immediately. For urgent but non-life-threatening situations, call our 24/7 support line at (555) 123-4567. Our medical team will assess your situation and provide guidance.
